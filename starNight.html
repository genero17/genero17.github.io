A blast from the past

ABES EC witnessed a live concert in its campus on the final day of Genero'16. The famous Punjabi Singer 'Guru Randhawa' was booked for the concert through the company 'Grog Events'. The show was well managed by the disciple team of students and faculty. Passes were provided for every student and also to VIP's. The show witnessed the largest gathering of about 4500+ audience. It was also the most successful and enjoyable event of the fest.
